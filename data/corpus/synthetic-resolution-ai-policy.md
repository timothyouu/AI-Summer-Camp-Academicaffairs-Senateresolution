# Demo stand-in: generative AI policy

This document is synthetic and exists only to demonstrate resolution checking. It has not been adopted by CSUB or its Academic Senate.

A future university policy should require transparent disclosure of material generative-AI assistance, protect confidential student and personnel information, preserve faculty responsibility for academic judgment, and provide an accessible alternative when an AI-enabled tool cannot be used equitably. Implementation guidance should distinguish teaching, research, administration, and personnel-review contexts.
