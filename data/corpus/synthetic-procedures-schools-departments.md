# Demo stand-in: schools and departments

This synthetic document demonstrates overlap detection and has no policy authority. A proposal to create, rename, reorganize, or discontinue a school or academic department should identify the academic rationale, affected programs and employees, consultation record, resource implications, accreditation effects, and proposed implementation date. The final route and approval authority must be verified in current campus policy.
