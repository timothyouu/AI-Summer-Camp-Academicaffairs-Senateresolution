# Prior-service credit

For the demo corpus, this reviewed excerpt records that a probationary faculty appointment may include up to two years of credit for prior service. The written appointment record controls whether credit was granted and the amount of credit. This excerpt is a searchable aid; reviewers should consult the supplied University Handbook PDF for the controlling language.

This material aligns with Unit 3 CBA Article 13.4 on the two-year ceiling. It is intentionally not labeled a conflict.
