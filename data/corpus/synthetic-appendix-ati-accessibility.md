# Accessibility review aid

The supplied Handbook's accessibility materials require attention to accessible instructional materials and technology. When a technology is not accessible, an equally effective alternative access plan is needed. This reviewed search aid summarizes the supplied source for retrieval; the Handbook PDF remains the authoritative document.

Accessibility should be considered during selection and adoption, not added only after a student reports a barrier. Procurement, instructional use, and remediation decisions should be documented with the responsible campus offices.
