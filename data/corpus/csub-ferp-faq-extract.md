# FERP FAQ retrieval extract

This searchable extract is based on the user-supplied CSUB FERP FAQ and is guidance subject to change. Campus guidance identifies February 15 as the operational notice deadline for entering FERP and advises faculty to coordinate the request with the President, Provost, Dean, and Faculty Affairs. The Unit 3 CBA's controlling formulation is written notice at least six months before the academic year, subject to a discretionary presidential waiver.

FERP work must satisfy Article 29 appointment limits and CalPERS retired-annuitant rules. A participant should not treat the CBA's 90-workday or 50-percent limit and the CalPERS 960-hour limit as alternatives; the appointment must satisfy every applicable restriction. Individual eligibility and exceptions, including the 180-day waiting-period exception for qualifying CSU FERP participation, should be confirmed with Faculty Affairs and CalPERS.
